How to Prepare for a Puja

January 5, 2024
Preparing for a puja involves both physical and mental preparation. Here&apos;s a guide to help you get ready for your ceremony. Physical Preparation: - Clean your home or puja space - Gather necessary items as per the service requirements - Arrange seating for participants Mental Preparation: - Set your intention for the ceremony - Approach the ritual with devotion and respect - Follow any pre-ceremony observances recommended The priest will provide a detailed list of items needed for your specific ceremony when you book.
